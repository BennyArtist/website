About Benny

Stage name: Benny. Born January 13, 2001. From a young age she discovered music and at about 14 she began learning bass guitar and attending vocal training. Her music bridges genres — Dancehall, RnB, Hip-hop, Pop and Afro — and her craft is a voice of modern Malawian creativity.

This personal site highlights the artist behind the business: her story, her music, and the handmade vision of KAMWAAMBE.
